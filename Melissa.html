Melissas FAVORITE Crockpot Recipes

A Little about My Page

These recipes have been shared between myself and my sister in-law Shanea Johnson who lives in chattaway, BC. We are both learning as we go along and these are just some of the ones we have perfected. Enjoy!

Slow Cooker Chilli

1 lb of ground beef or pork browned

1/2 diced white onion

3 cloves garlic minced

1 green pepper diced

1 cup of frozen corn (optional)

1 large can of red kidney beans drained and rinsed

1 large can of diced tomatoes

2 cups of water

1 cup of beef broth

2 small cans of tomato paste

3 Tbsp of chilli powder

2 Tbsp of taco seasoning

Cook on medium for a minimum of 2 hours for best flavour

Loaded Potatoes

Coat bottom and sides of slow cooker with cooking spray

2 lbs of baby potatoes cut in half or in quarters if large

2 Tbsp of olive oil

1 cup of cooked, chopped bacon

1/2 a yellow onion finely chopped

1/2 Tsp of paprika

1 Tsp of dried parsley

Mix all together well and top with 1 cup of shredded cheese

Cook on low for 4 hours

Pulled Pork

1 Pork shoulder roast or loin

1 Onion chopped

2 Cloves of minced garlic

2 Tsp of Steak spice

Sprinkle of salt and pepper

1 Cup of your favorite BBQ sauce

1/2 Cup of chicken broth

Potato Bacon Soup

3 Diced potatoes

1/2 an onion

1 diced onion

2 stalks of celery diced

1/2 Bacon fried and cut up

2 cans of mushroom soup

2 Cups of milk

1 cup chicken broth

Cook on low heat for minimum of 5 hours.

Serve on a bun with coleslaw or with your favorite greens
